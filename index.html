Know our
Global Community

Learning About Our Global Community takes young readers on a journey beyond their own communities to learn about aspects of daily life in other places around the world. Inviting text, accessible maps, and vibrant photographs work together to explain how cultural and environmental characteristics affect people's lives and help determine how they meet their needs.
